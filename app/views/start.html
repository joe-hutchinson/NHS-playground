Learning Support Fund - Child Dependants Allowance

  1. What you'll get

What you'll get:

Eligible students attending a full time pre-registration healthcare course will be able to claim a set amount of £1,000.00 per academic year.

This allowance will not affect your access to child support or other means of funding through your higher education provider or other sources.

Payments will be made in three termly instalments:

  • term 1 - £250
  • term 2 - £250
  • term 3 - £500

Check your eligibility:

Child Dependants Allowance is available to new students who have parental responsibility for a child who is either:

  • under the age of 15 years
  • under 17 years if the child is registered with special educational needs

Parental responsibility means someone who is either a parent or has the same legal rights, duties, powers and responsibilities as a parent for a child.

This allowance will not affect your access to child support or other means of funding through your higher education provider or other sources.

How to claim

You need to apply for this service online.

You might need to phone the service helpline if you struggle to apply for this service online.

Start now

What you need to apply

You’ll need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone number
  • an address
  • your National insurance number

In addition you may need:

  • the name of the DWP/HMRC benefits you receive
  • information about DWP/HMRC benefits you your partner or your carer receive
  • if you are pregnant, the due date of your pregnancy
  • if you have children, the children's names and dates of birth

    • If you do not provide the right information when you apply it might affect when you get your benefit or how much you get.
